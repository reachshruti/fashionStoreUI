About Indo-Saaj

Summer of 2015, Swati Agarwal graduated from the National Institute of Fashion Technology, India with many awards. Five months later, Swati started her eponymous label which began with a workforce of just two persons. Over the year, she has successfully built a clothing brand which has a very strong social perspective. Quick to understand the demands of the Indian market, Swati has created a brand that has represented a comfort zone to global Indians and remained undestroyed through major socio-economical-political changes.
